History panel

This panel holds the historical resume of the application's jobs.



Jobs history stored inside the database are here listed. Various interactions may be realized :

  • Display a job information by selecting its row.
  • Display a job trigger result by once again selection its row.
  • Reset the job as if it hadn't run yet by selecting said job and clicking on the 'Re-process' button.
  • Deleting a selection of jobs by clicking on the 'Clear' button. (All dependencies will be removed aswell)
